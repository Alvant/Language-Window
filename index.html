The Course of Languge Acquisition

Age Description
~ 1 Single words: mama, car, dog, eat, yes, no, hi
~ 1-2 Simple phrases: all dry, more cereal, no bed, give doggie, tractor go floor
~ 2-3 Bloom: syntactic types number increases exponentially
~ 4 Almost all parts of the language are acquired
